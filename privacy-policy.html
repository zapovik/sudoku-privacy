Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how Sudoku ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information.

Data We Collect

NO PERSONAL DATA COLLECTION

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration required
  • No email addresses collected
  • No names or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No browsing history
  • No IP addresses

All game data is stored locally on your device only.

Local Storage

The following data is stored ONLY on your device:

  • Game progress and current state
  • Statistics (games played, win rate, time)
  • User preferences (theme, language, sound)
  • Campaign progress

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Analytics

ANONYMOUS USAGE DATA ONLY

We may collect anonymous, aggregated analytics to improve the app:

  • App crashes and errors
  • Feature usage (which buttons are clicked)
  • Game difficulty preferences

This data is:

  • Completely anonymous
  • Cannot be linked to you
  • Used only for app improvement

You can opt out of analytics in Settings.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services that collect data, including:

  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No payment processors (app is free)
  • No analytics beyond Apple's built-in tools

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for all ages, including children under 13.

Since we don't collect any personal information, there is no risk to children's privacy. Parents can feel confident letting their children use this app.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no data to:

  • Access
  • Correct
  • Delete
  • Export

You have full control over your data by managing it directly on your device.

Data Security

All data is stored securely on your device using iOS's built-in security features:

  • Encrypted storage (iOS Keychain for sensitive data)
  • Protected by your device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
  • Never transmitted over the internet

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on our website.

Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
